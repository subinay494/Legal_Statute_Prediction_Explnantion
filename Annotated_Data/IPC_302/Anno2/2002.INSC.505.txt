Briefly stated prosecution case is that <S3>due to quarrel between him and his</S3>
<S3>wife, the appellant had set her on fire after beating her and pouring kerosene</S3>
<S3>on 14.6.1997 at their residence, consequent to which she died on 15.6.1997 in</S3>
<S3>the hospital.</S3> After the incident in question, it is the prosecution case that

<S3>husband was quarrelling with her and had beaten her the previous night and on</S3>

<S3>poured kerosene and burnt her with a match stick and her clothes started</S3>
<S3>burning and her body got burnt.</S3> She has also stated that she started shouting

She has also stated that she is an illiterate.
