Filtering out unnecessary details, the prosecution version as unfolded during
trial is as follows:

<S3>On 31.12.1988, there was an altercation between Jairam Das and Sadananda</S3>
<S3>(hereinafter referred to as the deceased) on one hand and Jagabandhu Samal</S3>
<S3>(D.W.1) on the other near Motto Hat in connection with occupation/construction</S3>
<S3>of a shed in a market area.</S3>

When Jagabandhu suddenly got up his head struck against a bamboo protruding
into the thatch and he sustained some injury. Subsequently, when Jairam Das
(PW-1), Gagan Das (PW-5) and <S3>the deceased proceeded towards their village near</S3>
<S3>Balabhadrapur Sasan, they found that the accused persons armed with lathi,</S3>
<S3>tentas etc. were coming.</S3>

Being afraid, the deceased and his companions ran towards to the village.
Gagan Das (P.W. 5) went inside the house of Sikhar Bal whereas the other three
concealed themselves inside the house of Nilakantha Rath (P.W. 8). The house
was surrounded by the accused persons who dealt blows on the door and walls of
the house and some of them entered inside the house. <S3>Accused Panchanan</S3>
<S3>(appellant 10 in the present appeal) and Subash Samal (appellant 7 in the</S3>
<S3>present appeal) dragged the three persons and assaulted them.</S3> At that time,
one of the accused persons shouted that police personnel were coming and
subsequently all the accused persons fled away. PW-5 who saw the incident
through an opening in the door leaf of the house of Sikhar Bal lodged the
report before the Police which was treated as the First Information Report.
Apart from PW-1 who was injured in the incident and PW-5, the informant, the
occurrence was seen by some other persons including PWs. 2, 3, 4, 7 and 8. The
last two witnesses are the owners of the house wherein the deceased and his
companions had taken refuge and also spoke about the occurrence but except a
few they were not able to name the other accused persons. Investigation was
undertaken and on completion thereof, charge sheet was placed.
