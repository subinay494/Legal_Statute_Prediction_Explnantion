The fact that there was a fight in the morning of 3.11.1995 in regard to
collecting the water from the tap between the deceased and PW-2 Harinder Singh
on one side and the appellant on the other is an admitted fact. Pursuant to
the said fight, it is also admitted by the appellant that he went to his house
and brought his licenced gun and started walking towards the house of the
Pradhan of the Village.

Then the prosecution contends, the appellant after getting the gun, went to
the house of the deceased where he was locked in a room of his house by his
family members to prevent any further fight, <S3>there the appellant shot the
deceased through the window, consequent to which he died.</S3> The appellant
contends that when he was walking to the house of Pradhan, PW-2 confronted him
and tried to snatch his gun, consequent to which there was a scuffle and the
gun discharged accidentally, and in that process, the bullet went through the
window and hit the deceased which caused his death, therefore, he was not
responsible for his death.
