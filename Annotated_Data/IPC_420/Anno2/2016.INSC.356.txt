Respondents nos.4 to 7 herein describing themselves as members of the All Kerala Chemists and Druggists Association (hereinafter referred to as `the Association’), filed a written complaint to the City Police Commissioner, Ernakulam against respondent nos.1 to 3. Respondent No.1 – Mohana Kurup was the President of the Association during the relevant period from 2004 to 2006 and thereafter from 2006 to 2008. Respondent No.2 – Raveendran was the Secretary of the Association during the same period, and respondent no.3 – Sayed was the Treasurer of the Association during the relevant period. 
<S5>It was alleged in the complaint filed by respondent nos.4 to 7, that respondent nos.1
to 3, in furtherance of a criminal conspiracy, and with common intention, misappropriated huge amounts of funds of `the Association', by misusing their position as office bearers of `the Association'.</S5>
