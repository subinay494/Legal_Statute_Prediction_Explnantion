The respondent initially started his career as a sweeper in the Bank of India, Basta Branch on 1978 and rose ultimately to the post of daftry.
At the relevant time, the respondent was working in Akhupada Branch of the Bank.
<S5>While so working, a report came to be made against him that he had tried to defraud the bank by attempting to withdraw rs.
35,000/- allegedly deposited in the name of one Babaji Nayak.</S5>
The prosecution for the offences under Sections 419/420/468 Indian Penal Code, 1860 came to be launched against him and he was convicted by the trial court for the offence punishable under Section 468 Indian Penal Code, 1860 alone though he was acquitted of the other offences.
