Further case of the prosecution is that before marriage of the deceased with the appellant, negotiations were held, wherein the appellant had <S6> demanded dowry in the form of cash amounting to Rs 15,000/- as well as gold and silver ornaments </S6>.
After marriage, the deceased had been subjected to <S6> mental and physical torture </S6> over certain issues including the one for demand of balance of dowry.
The deceased had complained to her brother and sister as well as to her brother-in-law about the ill-treatment meted out on her by the appellant and ultimately, the deceased being unable to bear any more torture of the appellant, <S6> committed suicide by setting herself on fire in their house at about 5:00 AM on 16-3-1990 i.e., within a year of marriage </S6>.
