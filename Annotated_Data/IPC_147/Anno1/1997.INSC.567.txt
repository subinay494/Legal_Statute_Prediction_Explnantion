<S1>In all 14 accused were tried for committing murder of one Veerabhadram of Kamanchikal Village and causing hurt to his wife Saraswati, in the Court of the Sessions Judge Khammam, in Sessions Case no.121 of It was the prosecution case that Village Kamanchikal is divided into two political factions.</S1>
Veerabhadram belonged to the Communist Party of India and the accused belonged to the opposite faction CPI(M) and TDP.
There were previous clashes between the two factions which had led to filing of criminal cases and posting of a police party in that village.
On 1987 at about 6 a.m there was an attempt on the life of one P.
Anantharamulu alias Babu, brother of accused no.1 and a member of the CPI(M) on the outskirts of that village but towards adjoining village Ramannapet.
<S1>As a retaliatory measure A-1 along with A-2 to A-14 who belonged to CPI and TDP formed themselves into an unlawful assembly, the common object of which was to cause death of Veerabhadram (hereinafter referred to as the deceased).</S1>
<S1>Thereafter they went to the hut of the deceased armed with axes, knives, spears and sticks at about 7 a.m.</S1>
<S1>Seeing this mob of the accused two daughters of the deceased who were sitting outside the hut alerted him.</S1>
So he immediately bolted the door from inside.
<S1>after reaching there, A-1, A-2, A-4, A-6 and A-8 broke open the door and went inside the hut while others remained waiting outside in the courtyard.
The accused after entering the hut said that their party man was attacked and thereafter they by dragging the deceased took him out of the hut in the courtyard.
All the accused then started beating the deceased.
When Saraswati PW.1 wife of the deceased tried to intervene she was beaten by the stick portion of their spears by A-B and A- When PW.3 Venkatalaxmi and other minor daughter Vijaya tried to save their father they were pushed away by A- When Venkatakrishnan PW.2 and Aluri Raja PW.5 pleaded for mercy and tried to intervene they were threatened with serious consequences.
All the accused thereafter left that place believing that the deceased had died.
