<S1>The prosecution case briefly stated is that, on 1980 during night all the accused respondents being armed with gun, sticks and acid entered into the hut inside the field of Mangi Lal where Mangi Lal was sleeping.</S1>
<S1>Respondents then dragged him out of the hut and some of them assaulted him by sticks and respondent Chhagan Lal who was carrying a gun assaulted him with the butt of the gun.</S1>
Respondent - Mohan Lal threw acid on him.
Deceased - Mangi Lal shouted for help.
Having heard the noise, Abdul Rehulan, PW.
1 who was staying at a distance of 100 yards, woke up and ran towards the place from where Mohan Lal's voice was coming.
Reaching at the place of occurrence, when PW.
1 asked Mangi Lal about the incident, he told the names of the accused and told him that they have killed him by throwing acid.
Said PW.
1 then informed Mangi Lal's family members and soon Mangi Lal's son Ram Gopal, PW.
4 arrived at the place of occurrence.
Mangi Lal was then brought to the village in the injured condition and was carried on a tractor to Manasa Police Station.
Mangi Lal himself lodged the report, which was treated as F.I.R.
